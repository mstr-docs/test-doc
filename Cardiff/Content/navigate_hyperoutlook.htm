Navigate the HyperIntelligence for Office Add-in

The add-in appears in a panel on the right-hand side of Outlook. Click the thumbtack to keep the panel open when you click on different emails. Click the X icon to close the panel.

Filter Card Source

Click this icon to filter what objects in your email trigger cards. Select from email subject, sender, recipient, and/or the content in the body of the email.

Enable or Disable Cards

Click this icon and select the Cards tab to view the cards available in your environment. Use the toggles to enable or disable cards.

View Settings

Click this icon and select the Settings tab to clear your cache, log out, view the Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, or the HyperIntelligence for Office help.
