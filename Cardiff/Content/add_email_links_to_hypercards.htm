Add Email Links to Cards

When creating a HyperIntelligence dataset, you can create a column specifically for email addresses. Changing the data type in this column to Email allows card consumers to click or tap on an email address in the card to draft an email.

The default email software on your machine will launch with the email address.

How to Add Email Links to Your Dataset

  1. In your dataset, add a column containing a set of email addresses.
  2. Import your data to Workstation and click Preview Data.
  3. In the Preview dialog, right-click the email column and select Change Data Type > Email.

  4. Click Finish.

    Email address links are now embedded in cards for consumers to click or tap.
