Navigate HyperIntelligence for Mobile

Use the tabs in the HyperIntelligence for Mobile app to view and search for cards, see cards related to your calendar activities, or view your account settings.

  • iOS
  • Android

Home Tab

The Home tab displays a history of cards you've viewed in iOS Spotlight search, in-app search, and from your Calendar notifications. Type keywords into the in-app search to view cards.

Calendar Tab

The Calendar tab allows users to access cards that are matched to keywords in Today’s, Past, and Upcoming calendar events. Click on cards in your Calendar tab to view their contents.

Cards Tab

The Cards tab allows you to manage your cards. View your cards and tap the toggle to enable or disable them. All certified cards and cards hosted in the environment appear on this tab.

Account Tab

The Account tab provides user information. View logging information, send logs, view HyperIntelligence for Mobile help, the privacy policy, terms of use, or log out of your environment.
