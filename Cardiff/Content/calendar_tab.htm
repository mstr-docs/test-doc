View Calendar Tab

The Calendar tab allows you to access cards matched to keywords in Today’s, Past, and Upcoming Calendar events directly in the Hyper application.

  • iOS
  • Android

Enable Calendars

By default, HyperIntelligence for Mobile will scan all your calendars in the native Calendar application. If you've synced a third-party calendar application, like Gmail or Outlook, with your native Calendar application, you will also see calendars from the third-party applications.

To prevent HyperIntelligence for Mobile from matching cards with certain calendars, de-select calendars from the Account tab in the Hyper application.

How to Disable Calendars

  1. In HyperIntelligence for Mobile, tap the Account tab.
  2. Tap Enable Calendars.
  3. Tap the calendar to de-select it from Enabled Calendars. De-selecting all calendars will cause the Calendar tab to be empty.

Filter the Card Source

As HyperIntelligence for Mobile scans your calendar events for keywords, it is reviewing three aspects of the event: the event title, attendees, and event notes. These aspects are called the source.

To modify which source is producing keyword matches, de-select any of the three sources.

How to De-Select a Source

  1. In HyperIntelligence for Mobile, tap the Calendar tab.
  2. Tap the Filter Sourceicon.
  3. Tap the source to de-select it. De-selecting all sources will cause the Calendar tab to be empty.
