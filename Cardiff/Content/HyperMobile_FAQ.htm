HyperIntelligence for Mobile FAQ (iOS)

Use this document to find answers to the most commonly asked questions about HyperIntelligence for Mobile (iOS specifically).

For more detailed information, and steps to set up the app on your device, see our HyperIntelligence for Mobile product documentation.

  1. What is HyperIntelligence for Mobile?

    HyperIntelligence for Mobile is an application that allows you to view and interact with cards on both iOS and Android devices. With features such as iOS Spotlight Search, calendar-based notifications, and a new Calendar tab, you can search for a specific card in your environment's database, enable your device to send push notifications with relevant cards, or view card related to your up coming calendar events.

  2. What devices are HyperIntelligence for Mobile available on?

    As of MicroStrategy 2019 Update 2, the app is available on both iOS and Android devices.
  3. What versions of iOS does HyperIntelligence for Mobile support?

    This app operates on iOS 11 or later.
  4. How does HyperIntelligence for Mobile work?

    This app enables you to view cards by connecting to the environment your cards were built in. Unlike the HyperIntelligence for Web, keywords are not underlined in your mobile device. However, cards are still fully accessible via search (either in-app or Spotlight for iOS) or notifications.
  5. What features are available on HyperIntelligence for Mobile?
    • Search for Cards:

      To find specific cards, you can use either the search feature built into HyperIntelligence for Mobile, or the iOS Spotlight Search feature on your iPad or iPhone. For instructions on how to use these features, see How to Search for Cards.

    • Push Notifications:

      For HyperIntelligence for Mobile for iOS, you can use the iOS Calendar app, which come out of the box with your iPhone or iPad, to send relevant cards alongside push notifications from your calendar. For HyperIntelligence for Mobile for Android, you can use any calendar app on your mobile device to send relevant cards. For instructions on how to use these features, see How to Enable Card Notifications.

    • Calendar Tab:

      The Calendar tab is a new addition to HyperIntelligence for Mobile for iOS. As of MicroStrategy 2019 Update 3, this tab allows you to access cards matched to keywords in Today’s, Past, and Upcoming iOS Calendar events directly in the Hyper application. For more information, see View Calendar Tab.
  6. How do I configure HyperIntelligence for Mobile?

    To configure the app, you must connect to an environment. Connecting to an environment can be done once you have the HyperIntelligence for Mobile Configuration URL sent by your admin. For more instructions, see How to Install and Configure HyperIntelligence for Mobile.
  7. Why don't my keyword searches return any results?

    Try the following steps if your searches don't return a card:

    1. Open the Cards tab in HyperIntelligence for Mobile.
    2. Ensure your cards are enabled. Then use the toggles to turn them off and back on.

  9. Can I connect HyperIntelligence for Mobile to multiple environments?

    HyperIntelligence for Mobile can only connect to one environment at a time. However, the app will remember previously configured environments.

  10. What are the security implications?

    HyperIntelligence for Mobile is built on the MicroStrategy platform and is held to the same security and governance standards as all our products. Any data shown through the app is only available if a user has successfully authenticated into the MicroStrategy platform.
  11. Why can't I see an image on a profile card?

    If images do not appear on a profile card, they're most likely unreachable. Please contact the card's author to resolve the issue.
  12. Why don't I see Calendar-based notifications?

    For HyperIntelligence for Mobile for iOS, ensure that you are scheduling events in the Calendar app that comes out of the box with iOS devices. Third-party calendar apps will not trigger cards.

    Additionally, the syncing of calendar events with the HyperIntelligence for Mobile app is controlled by iOS. If you create an event for the near future, the background sync may not occur in time to get the card notification.
  13. How recent is my card's data?

    Data is updated whenever the app is launched. You can refresh the data when using the app at any time by pulling down on your screen in the Cards tab.
  14. How do Calendar-based notifications work?

    Once permitted, HyperIntelligence for Mobile scans the subject and attendees of a calendar event and uses that information to present relevant cards.

    Calendar-based notifications may be delayed on iOS devices depending on how often your device syncs with third-party apps. To ensure that HyperIntelligence for Mobile iOS still sends cards for recently scheduled calendar events, open the app as needed to automatically sync it with your calendar.

    Android devices automatically sync with third-party apps, so HyperIntelligence for Mobile will be able to receive your latest calendar events and send cards accordingly.
